Created: May 10, 2022 Last Updated April 3, 2023: Here are answers to frequently asked questions about my first game, Autopanic! What is Autopanic? 🔗Autopanic is fully diegetic twinstick shooter with rogue-like elements in which you need to follow the guidance of an AI to reach the top floor of a mysteries facility. Autopanic aims to deliver some interesting narrative design along with satisfying fast-paced combat. Rogue-like elements will provide some variance during gameplay and you need to fully learn the mechanics to make it to the end.
