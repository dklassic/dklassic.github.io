*Last Updated November 25, 2025:* Here are answers to frequently asked questions about my game, ***All You Need Is Rush***!

# What is *All You Need Is Rush* about?

***All You Need Is Rush*** is a narritve-focused short experimental incremental game, with gameplay mechanics designed with storytelling in mind. Featuring a stamina managing gameplay inspired by minesweeper.

# Where can I get *All You Need Is Rush*?

***All You Need Is Rush*** will soon be available on:

- [Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3237840/)

# What are the minimum system requirements?

Let's just say if you got a computer more powerful than Steam Deck you'll be running it just fine.

For Steam Deck the game targets 800p/90FPS.

# Does *All You Need Is Rush* have PC controller support?

Yep.

# Does *All You Need Is Rush* support PC cloud saves?

Yes it does! You may also find local copies of your saves under `Documents/Saved Games/All You Need Is Rush`.
